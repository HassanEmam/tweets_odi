This initiative aims at producing publicly available data for construction industry by utilising people contributions to labelling and tagging different dataset. I am pleased you are contributing to generating a dataset that is relevant to construction projects. Please contact me if you do have a dataset that you wish to tag or share with the industry. For this dataset you are required to classify each tweet with three different variables:
  1. Sentiment: whether it is supporting neutral or against the project scheme
  2. Sarcasm: one of the big issues in data analytics if there is sarcasm in the tweet AI can classify sentiment incorrectly. So, please also tag whether a tweet has sarcasm in it or not.
  3. relevance: is the tweet relevant to the construction project
